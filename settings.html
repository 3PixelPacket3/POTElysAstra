Manual Cloud Synchronization

Your app automatically syncs with the EAHA central servers on startup. However, you can click the button below to force a manual pull. This will download any new rules or base creature stat updates published by the Admin without overwriting your personal modifications.

Danger Zone: Clear Local Cache

This will purge your device's high-capacity IndexedDB cache. It will not delete your cloud profile, but it will force the app to completely rebuild its database from the cloud servers upon your next login. Use this if your app is experiencing severe desynchronization.
