Backup Best Practices

  • Weekly Exports: Click "Export Backup" once a week to download a `.json` file to your computer.
  • Cross-Device Syncing: To use your EAHA dashboard on another computer or phone, export the JSON file, send it to your other device, and click "Import Backup".
  • Corrupted Data: If your lists stop loading, click "Factory Reset" to clear the cache and reload the staging data.
