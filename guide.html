Welcome to EAHA

The Elys Astra Helper Application is an offline, browser-based toolkit designed specifically for players and staff of the Elys Astra Path of Titans server. It centralizes your survival tracking, post formatting, rule lookup, combat analysis, and tactical mapping into one seamless dashboard.

⚠️ Crucial: Data Storage & Updates

EAHA operates 100% offline. Everything you type, create, or map is stored directly inside your browser's "Local Storage." If you clear your browser cache, you will lose your personal data. The JSON Backup tab is your command center for managing this.

  • Export Personal Backup: Downloads your entire application state (Rules, Maps, Combat Logs, Custom Stats) to a file. Do this frequently.
  • Import Personal Backup: Restores the application to your exact personal state from a previously downloaded file.
  • Check for App Updates (Merge): When server staff push a new update, click this. The system will download the newest official Rules and Creature Profiles and merge them into your app without deleting your personal map markers, custom stat tweaks, or combat logs.
  • Factory Reset: Wipes all local browser data and rebuilds the app strictly from the official base file. This deletes your personal logs, so ensure you have exported a personal backup first.

Comprehensive Manual

1. Elysian Dashboard & Lifeline

Your primary control center for active survival tracking.

  • Active Dinosaur Selection: Upon loading, the dashboard defaults to an unassigned state. Use the dropdown to select a creature from your database to load its specific upkeep tasks, diets, and habitats.
  • Vitals & Upkeep Timer: Track your Comfort, Hygiene, and Satiation. Enter a minute value and click "Start Decay" to have your vitals drain proportionally. Use the Reset Timer button to safely halt the countdown without wiping your current vital percentages.
  • Eldering Progress: Track your !migrationbuff milestones. The UI will update to show you the current stage and alert you when Rebirth tokens are unlocked at 16 migrations.
  • Quick Commands: Click any command chip to instantly copy it to your clipboard. Toggle "Edit Mode" to delete commands, or use the input box to add new ones.

2. Post Builder

A drag-and-drop formatting engine for Discord announcements and LFG posts.

  • Cookie-Cutter Templates: Select a pre-built layout (like Event Setup or Nesting Request) to instantly populate the form.
  • Design Library: Click bubbly chips to inject custom dividers, server symbols, and status emojis directly into your text.
  • Rich Formatting: Highlight text and use the toolbar to apply Discord markdown (Bold, Spoilers) or convert your text into unique Unicode fonts (Gothic, Script).

3. Rules & Guidelines

Your server's offline encyclopedia and moderation tool.

  • Fast Search: Search by rule title or keyword tags (e.g., "body down") to instantly pull up rulings.
  • Rich Editing: Use the rich text editor to format rules with bolding, italics, and bullet points.

4. Creature Profiles & Stats

Maintain detailed lore, biology, and statistical data for every dinosaur.

  • Magic Auto-Fill (OCR): Drag and drop (or Ctrl+V) a screenshot of a creature's profile card from Discord. The AI will scan the image, auto-crop the avatar to a perfect square, and automatically extract the Name, Tier, Diet, Habitats, Upkeep tasks, and Behavior Titles into the form.
  • Custom Mechanics: Add customized stat fields (e.g., "Bleed Heal Rate" or "Bonebreak Resist") to any creature to track specialized mechanics.
  • Stat Calculator: Navigate to the Stats tab to view a creature's base values. Use the Sandbox Calculator to safely test percentage buffs (e.g., +15% Health) without permanently altering the database.

5. Combat Log

Track your PvP history, identify dangerous map zones, and analyze hunting efficiency.

  • Quick Logging: Use the lightning-fast Quick Log section on the dashboard to record a win, loss, or starvation event in one click. It automatically associates the event with your currently active dinosaur.
  • Isolated Analytics: Use the "All My Dinosaurs" dropdown filter in the sidebar to isolate your dashboard charts. You can view exactly how many kills or deaths a specific dinosaur in your roster has accumulated.
  • Deployments Chart: A dedicated bar chart tracks which dinosaurs you deploy (and die/kill with) the most across your entire roster.

6. Tactical Map

Interactive map of Gondwa for deploying tactical markers.

  • Navigation: Scroll your mouse wheel to zoom in/out, and click-and-drag to pan across the map.
  • Recon Sync Radar: Paste (Ctrl+V) a fully zoomed-out screenshot of your in-game map into the tool. When the radar overlay appears, click directly on your player arrow within the image to calculate the precise coordinates and deploy a marker exactly where you are standing. The system automatically corrects for widescreen aspect ratios.
  • Marker Editing: Click and drag any marker on the map to reposition it. Click the "✎" icon in the sidebar list to quickly rename it or change its category.

Troubleshooting

Q: My lists are empty and nothing is saving!
A: Go to the JSON Backup tab and click "Factory Reset". This forces the app to rebuild its database from the default `JSON.json` file included in your folder.

Q: Why isn't my Dashboard timer making a sound?
A: The timer relies on standard browser notifications. If you denied notification permissions when your browser prompted you, the alarm will only trigger visually on the screen. Ensure browser notifications are allowed for your local file path.

Q: I pasted a map snip, but clicking does nothing.
A: Ensure you are clicking directly on the screenshot inside the popup modal. The system needs you to pinpoint the exact location of your arrow to calculate the X/Y percentages.

Q: My OCR Auto-Fill is extracting weird symbols or merging text poorly.
A: The OCR engine reads pixels and strips out bot artifacts (like `~` or `@`). If your Discord screenshot includes heavy background UI or reactions, crop your screenshot tighter around the main text block before pasting it in.
