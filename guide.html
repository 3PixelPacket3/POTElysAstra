Welcome to EAHA

The Elys Astra Helper Application is an offline, browser-based toolkit designed specifically for players and staff of the Elys Astra Path of Titans server. It centralizes your survival tracking, post formatting, rule lookup, and statistical analysis into one seamless dashboard.

⚠️ Crucial: How Data Saving Works

EAHA operates 100% offline. It does not connect to an external server or a cloud database. Instead, everything you type, create, or save is stored directly inside your browser's "Local Storage."

  • The Risk: If you clear your browser cache/cookies, or use an Incognito/Private window, your data will be permanently wiped.
  • The Solution: You must regularly use the JSON Backup tab to export your data to a file on your computer.
  • Cross-Device: To use your EAHA setup on your phone or another PC, export your JSON backup from your main computer, send it to your other device, and use the "Import" button.

Module Breakdown

1. Elysian Dashboard

Your primary control center. Select an active dinosaur from your database to load its specific upkeep tasks. Use the decay timer to alert you when your Comfort, Hygiene, or Satiation reaches 0%. Also features one-click Quick Commands for Discord.

2. Post Builder

A drag-and-drop formatting engine for Discord. Use Cookie-Cutter templates to instantly generate event posts, LFG requests, or staff announcements. The live preview automatically formats Discord markdown (like bolding and blockquotes).

3. Rules & Guidelines

A fast-search directory for server moderation. Type a keyword like "body down" to instantly pull up the relevant ruling. You can edit, format, and tag rules to build a comprehensive server encyclopedia.

4. Creature Profiles & Stats

Your master database. Any creature added here will automatically appear in the Dashboard dropdown. The Stats tab allows you to view precise combat metrics and use a sandbox calculator to test percentage buffs and nerfs safely.

Troubleshooting

Q: My lists are empty and nothing is saving!
A: Go to the JSON Backup tab and click "Factory Reset". This forces the app to rebuild its database from the default `base-data.json` file included in your folder.

Q: Why isn't my Dashboard timer making a sound?
A: The timer relies on standard browser notifications. If you denied notification permissions when your browser prompted you, the alarm will only trigger visually on the screen.
